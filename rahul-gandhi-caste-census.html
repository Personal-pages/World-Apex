Rahul Gandhi Calls Opposition to Caste Census ‘Anti-National’.

Published: March 20, 2025 | 10:47 PM

campaign Rahul Gandhi Pushes for Caste Census

Congress leader Rahul Gandhi has criticized those opposing the caste census, calling it an “anti-national” stance. He emphasized that a caste-based census is necessary for achieving social justice and equitable distribution of resources.

gavel Why is Rahul Gandhi Advocating for a Caste Census?

bar_chart Addressing Social Inequality: He believes a caste census would reveal the true socioeconomic conditions of marginalized communities and ensure fair policymaking.

group Strengthening Welfare Policies: The data could help the government allocate better job reservations, educational opportunities, and economic support to backward classes.

warning Criticism from Political Rivals

thumb_down Opposition from BJP: The ruling party has accused Rahul Gandhi of playing vote-bank politics, arguing that a caste census could divide society instead of uniting it.

question_mark Challenges in Implementation: Critics say that conducting a caste census nationwide would be logistically complex and could create regional tensions.

poll Public Reaction & Support

support Support from Regional Parties: Leaders from states like Bihar and Tamil Nadu have backed the initiative, saying it will empower lower castes.

emoji_people Mixed Public Response: While many from backward communities support it, others worry about potential political misuse of the data.

hourglass_empty What’s Next?

account_balance Congress has vowed to include the caste census in its manifesto for the upcoming elections. The government’s decision on whether to approve the census remains uncertain.

celebration A Historic Step? If implemented, the caste census could reshape India’s reservation policies and redefine political narratives across the country.
