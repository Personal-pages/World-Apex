Civilian ‘Thrashed’ by Army Officer During Search Operations in Rajouri; Army Orders Inquiry

Published: April 18, 2025 | 2:45 PM

security Incident and Immediate Reaction

A shocking incident has come to light in Rajouri, where a civilian was allegedly assaulted by an Army officer during a search operation. The incident has sparked widespread condemnation, with many questioning the behavior of military personnel during such sensitive operations.

report Army's Response and Inquiry

In response to the incident, the Army has swiftly ordered a formal inquiry into the matter. The inquiry aims to investigate the circumstances of the altercation and hold accountable those responsible for any misconduct during the operation.

question_answer Public Outcry and Media Coverage

The incident has generated significant media coverage, with public outcry calling for justice. Activists and citizens have demanded greater accountability for military actions, particularly in regions experiencing heightened security operations.
