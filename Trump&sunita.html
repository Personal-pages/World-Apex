Trump Explains Why He Hasn’t Invited Sunita Williams to the White House Yet

Published: March 19, 2025 | 02:44 PM

Donald Trump, in a recent statement, clarified why Indian-American astronaut Sunita Williams has not yet been invited to the White House. He mentioned that her commitments with NASA and continuous space missions have made scheduling a meeting difficult. Trump emphasized his admiration for Williams’ contributions to space exploration and assured that she will be honored appropriately at a suitable time. He added that recognizing astronauts’ achievements is a priority, and the delay is not due to a lack of appreciation but rather logistical constraints. As Williams continues her missions, Trump stated that the invitation will be extended when the timing aligns with both her and the White House’s schedule.
