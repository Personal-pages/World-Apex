Deepika Padukone, Amitabh Bachchan-starrer 'Piku' to Re-Release in May

Published: April 10, 2025 | 10:08 PM

celebration A Beloved Classic Returns to the Big Screen

The heartfelt 2015 drama *Piku*, which beautifully explored the relationship between an aging father and his daughter, is set for a grand re-release on May 9, 2025. This comes in celebration of the film’s 10th anniversary.

movie Star-Studded Cast

Starring Deepika Padukone, Amitabh Bachchan, and the late Irrfan Khan, *Piku* struck a chord with audiences and critics alike for its charming storytelling, emotional depth, and unforgettable performances.

favorite Deepika’s Emotional Tribute

Sharing the announcement on Instagram, Deepika wrote: "A film that will always have my heart – PIKU is back in theatres on 9th May, 2025 to celebrate its 10th Anniversary! Irrfan, we miss you! And think about you every so often..."

history_edu A Cinematic Gem

Directed by Shoojit Sircar, *Piku* became a cult classic for its warm portrayal of everyday struggles, love, and family bonds. The film’s sharp humor and poignant moments continue to resonate with audiences even a decade later.

event When and Where?

The film will be re-released in select theatres across India starting May 9, 2025. Film buffs and fans of the legendary trio are eagerly looking forward to reliving this emotional journey on the silver screen once again.

theater_comedy Legacy of Irrfan Khan

Irrfan’s soulful portrayal in *Piku* remains one of his most loved performances. The re-release is not just a celebration of the film, but also a tribute to his enduring legacy in Indian cinema.
