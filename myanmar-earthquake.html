Myanmar Hit by 5.1 Magnitude Earthquake Near Mandalay

Published: March 30, 2025 | 04:15 PM

public Myanmar Struck by Moderate Earthquake

A 5.1 magnitude earthquake struck near Mandalay in Myanmar on Sunday afternoon, causing panic among residents. The tremor was recorded at a depth of 10 km below the surface.
