Ananya Panday Becomes Chanel’s First-Ever Brand Ambassador from India

Published: April 10, 2025 | 10:32 PM

star Fashion Milestone for India

Bollywood actress Ananya Panday has made history by becoming Chanel’s first-ever brand ambassador from India. The French luxury label announced the collaboration in April 2025, marking a significant move that highlights the fashion house’s growing emphasis on global diversity.

check_circle Chanel Steps into India

This partnership is more than symbolic — it marks Chanel’s deeper engagement with India’s rapidly expanding luxury fashion market. Ananya’s youthful style and global presence make her a natural choice to represent the brand’s vision in South Asia.

mood Ananya’s Emotional Reveal

Taking to Instagram, Ananya shared her gratitude and joy: “Beyond grateful and excited for my journey with @chanelofficial. The first ever brand ambassador for and from India. Dreams really do come true.”

public Global Impact

The announcement was met with celebration across India and the global fashion community. Industry insiders say this move could pave the way for more Indian talent to shine on international platforms and red carpets.

photo_camera What’s Next?

As Chanel’s brand ambassador, Ananya is expected to represent the label at fashion weeks, international campaigns, and exclusive events, adding a fresh South Asian touch to the global fashion narrative.
